Mount Arjuna

I took pictures of Mount Arjuna from the top of Mount Buthak. Buthak is 2,868 meters tall and located in East Java, south of Mount Arjuno. People know these mountains for their challenging hiking trails and beautiful scenery. They are also important for their culture.

Super moon in 2024

I got this photo in 2024, during a Supermoon. A supermoon is the phenomenon of a full moon that looks bigger and brighter because it is at its closest point to Earth (perigee), creating a stunning and mesmerizing view of the sky.
